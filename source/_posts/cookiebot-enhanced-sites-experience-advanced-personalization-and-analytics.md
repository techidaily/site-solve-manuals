### Instant identity proofing and affirmation — anytime, anywhere.

In a digital world, identity matters. There’s a new victim of identity theft every two seconds. That's why financial services, insurance, government agencies and healthcare systems need an identity proofing and affirmation solution that's both secure for their business and easy for their customers to use.

68% of customers abandon online onboarding processes. How much lost revenue does this cost your organization? Not to mention the additional cost of using other channels for onboarding, new account opening, or enrollment. The ability to quickly and accurately verify and validate a customer's identity in real time is extremely important to the success of your process.

Join us for this webinar where we’ll present the challenges that industries such as financial services, insurance, government, healthcare and others face when onboarding new customers—and present a new solution for solving them.

We cover:

* How to engage customers via their preferred digital channel,
* How to reduce fraud and risk while improving overall outcomes of revenue acceleration,
* How to achieve customer satisfaction through automation.

By combining identity reading, verification, and validation along with advanced document processing, you can solve the challenge of effective onboarding. Join ABBYY as we present a better way to engage customers, prove their identity and verify the supporting documents and content required to deliver a frictionless experience.
